World's most Enlightened Beings

Sadhguru

A yogi and profound mystic of our times, is a visionary humanitarian and a prominent spiritual leader. A contemporary Guru, rooted as strongly in mundane and pragmatic matters as he is in inner experience and wisdom, Sadhguru works tirelessly towards the physical, mental, and spiritual well-being of all.

Eckhart Tolle

Born in Germany in 1948. He has been living for a long time in England and is now living in Canada. He is probably the most known living enlightened person in the world. He reached world fame with his book “The Power of Now” that became a New York Times bestseller after his appearance on the Oprah Winfrey Show.

Mooji

Born in Jamaica. He is still wearing the Rasta hairstyle, with the hair braided in the back, but he is living in Great Britain. Speaks in a very calm and peaceful way, looks like a Buddha with his little belly.

Gangaji

As a teacher and author Gangaji meets with seekers from across the globe and in all walks of life. She shares her direct experience of the essential message she received from her teacher Papaji and offers it to all who want to discover a true and lasting fulfillment.

Dolano

Redheaded German woman, who has been living in Poona, India since 1979. She practiced excessive meditation and came, after stays with Osho, Ponjaji and Gangaji to enlightenment. Her English sounds wooden and very German.

Isira Sananda

Good looking Australian. She had her first awakening with 16 while she was raped. In her death struggle she realized that the man raping her is pure love. From that moment on he refrained from her.
